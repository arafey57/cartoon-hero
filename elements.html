It is really so easy to brew Turkish tea when you follow these steps: Pour 1/4 cup of water in the small tea pot. ... Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 5 minutes so that the heat reaches the tea leaves in the small tea pot. Transfer more than half of the water in the large tea pot into the small teapot.
